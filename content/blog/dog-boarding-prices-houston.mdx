## Factors That Affect Dog Boarding Costs

Several elements determine **dog boarding prices**, ensuring facilities meet the diverse needs of pets and their owners.

### 1. Duration of Stay

- **Short Stays**: Ideal for overnight or weekend trips but may cost more per night.
- **Extended Stays**: Discounts are often available for stays exceeding a week​

### 2. Size and Breed of Dog

Large breeds may incur higher costs due to additional space and food requirements. Conversely, small dogs might fit into standard suites at a lower rate.

### 3. Seasonality

Holiday seasons typically see a surge in demand, leading to higher rates. Booking early can mitigate these costs​

### 4. Special Care Services

Medical requirements, anxiety management, or special diets may add to the total cost. Premium facilities often accommodate these needs for an additional fee​

## How to Prepare Your Dog for Boarding

Proper preparation ensures a stress-free experience for your pet.

### ● Familiarization

Schedule a short stay or daycare visit to help your dog acclimate to the boarding environment.

### ● Health Requirements

[Ensure vaccinations](http://www.nuvet.com/85413), including rabies and Bordetella, are current. Facilities like [The Dog House Pet Salon](https://www.thedoghouseps.com/) prioritize vaccinated pets for everyone’s safety.

### ● Packing Checklist

1. Food and medications (labeled with clear instructions).
2. Toys or comfort items.
3. Feeding and care schedules.

These steps help create a smooth transition and minimize anxiety during their stay.
