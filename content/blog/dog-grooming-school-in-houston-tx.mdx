## Why Choose a Career in Dog Grooming?

Dog grooming is a fulfilling career that combines a love for animals with the art of styling. It offers a unique blend of creativity and care, allowing groomers to enhance the well-being and appearance of pets while forming strong bonds with their furry clients. Professional groomers are always in demand, ensuring job stability and the opportunity to work in a variety of settings, from salons to mobile grooming units.

The demand for skilled groomers is on the rise, driven by the increasing number of pet owners in the United States. According to the American Pet Products Association (APPA), [66% of U.S. households, or about 86.9 million families, own a pet](https://www.iii.org/fact-statistic/facts-statistics-pet-ownership-and-insurance#:~:text=An%20estimated%20sixty%2Dsix%20percent,year%20the%20survey%20was%20conducted.). This surge in pet ownership has led to a growing market for pet grooming services. The global market for pet grooming services was valued at USD 7.45 billion in 2021. It is anticipated to grow at a compound [annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.3%, reaching USD 14.59 billion by 2030.](https://straitsresearch.com/report/pet-grooming-services-market#:~:text=The%20global%20pet%20grooming%20services,period%20(2022%E2%80%932030).)

Skilled groomers who can provide quality care and grooming services are essential to meeting this demand, making it an excellent time to pursue a career in dog grooming. As more people recognize the importance of regular grooming for their pets’ health and hygiene, the opportunities in this field continue to expand.

### Our Programs

We offer four specialized training programs to suit your career goals:

2. **The Basic Program**: An 8-week course that builds on the bather program with additional grooming techniques, including breed-specific cuts and styling.
3. **The Groomer Program**: A 12-week course that prepares you for advanced grooming tasks, such as hand-stripping and creative grooming. This program is ideal for those who want to become professional groomers.
4. **The Entrepreneur Program**: A 24-week comprehensive course that includes business operations training, customer service skills, and advanced grooming techniques. This program is designed for individuals who aspire to open their own grooming salon.
