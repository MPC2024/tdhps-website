## 1. Skin-First Grooming: Hypoallergenic Shampoos & Oatmeal Baths

Gentle care for sensitive skin is leading the grooming game in Galleria. Discover how we’re putting skin health first for happy, itch-free pets.

### Why “Skin-First” Matters in Houston’s Climate

Houston’s humidity, pollen, and city pollutants often lead to irritated skin, hotspots, and itching. That’s why we prioritize hypoallergenic dog grooming products in every grooming session.

### Trend Elements

- Hypoallergenic, plant-based shampoos that are sulfate- and paraben-free, with calming ingredients like chamomile and colloidal oatmeal
- Oatmeal and aloe soaks that reduce inflammation and help repair the skin
- Moisturizing balms made from jojoba and shea butter to lock in hydration without clogging pores

Pro Tip: Ask for the “Skin-First Express” add-on to treat problem areas in just 20 minutes

## 2. Eco-Chic Grooming: Sustainable, Low-Waste Practices

Going green isn’t just for humans—your pup’s grooming routine can be eco-friendly too. Explore how our sustainable choices make a big difference.

### Aligning with Galleria’s Green Lifestyle

In a community that values sustainability, we offer eco-conscious grooming services designed to reduce waste and carbon footprint.

### Green Innovations

- Refillable shampoo stations to reduce single-use plastics by up to 80%
- Grooming tools made from bamboo and recycled materials
- Compostable towels and wipes for eco-conscious clean-up

### How We Implement

All retail grooming products are available in bulk. We partner with vendors who use renewable energy, and our towel service uses eco-certified laundry cycles.
