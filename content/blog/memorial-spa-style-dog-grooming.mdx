## Local Factors: Why Memorial Park Dogs Need Extra TLC
Memorial Park pups lead active lives—chasing tennis balls by the waterway, rolling in grass under oak trees, and exploring shaded trails. Couple that with Houston’s heat, humidity, and high pollen counts, and you have:

- **Skin Irritants & Allergies:** Grass pollen and airborne allergens can penetrate coats, causing redness, itching, and hotspots.
- **Paw Pad Stress:** Rough terrain and hot sidewalks wear paw pads extra, leading to cracks or soreness.
- **Increased Dirt & Debris:** Trails and fields mean more dirt, sticks, and burrs embedded in fur—requiring deeper, gentler cleansing.

## At-Home Maintenance Between Spa Days

1. **Weekly Brushing** Removes loose hair and distributes natural oils. Use a soft-bristle or rubber brush.
2. **DIY Oatmeal Spray:** Mix ½ cup of colloidal oatmeal with water in a spray bottle—spritz on itchy spots.
3. **Paw Pad Balm:** Apply a thin layer of coconut oil or a pet-safe balm after walks to repair cracks.
4. **Quick Ear Wipe:** Use a damp cotton ball (never a Q-tip) with a mild ear cleanser to prevent build-up.
