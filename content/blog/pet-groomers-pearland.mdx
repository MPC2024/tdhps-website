## Why Grooming Matters

Pet grooming is an essential part of your dog’s overall health and well-being. Regular grooming helps:

- Detect skin issues, [parasites](https://bettervet.com/resources/pet-diseases/parasites-in-dogs), and potential health problems early.
- Reduce shedding and prevent matting that can lead to discomfort or infections.
- Maintain cleanliness and hygiene, ensuring your pet is comfortable and happy.
