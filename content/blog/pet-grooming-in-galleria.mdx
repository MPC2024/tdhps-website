## Tips for Maintaining a Happy Pet Between Grooming Sessions

While regular grooming appointments are vital, there are steps you can take at home to keep your pet happy and healthy between visits:

1. **Brush Regularly:** Prevent tangles and reduce shedding by brushing your pet’s coat.
2. **Check for Parasites:** Watch for ticks and fleas, especially after walks.
3. **Clean Ears and Eyes:** Clean your pet’s ears and eyes to avoid irritation or infection.
4. **Monitor Nail Growth:** Trim nails regularly to prevent discomfort and maintain proper paw health.

Following these simple tips can help prolong the benefits of your pet’s grooming session, ensuring they stay clean and comfortable.
